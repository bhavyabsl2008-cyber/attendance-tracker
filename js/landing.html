Features

Built around how students actually think

Not how university portals display data. Every feature answers a question you actually ask yourself.

01
Skip Calculator
Know the exact number of classes you can miss per subject while staying above the 75% threshold. Updated instantly as you enter new data.
02
End Semester Predictor
Best case and worst case attendance by June, based on remaining classes. Estimated at a realistic 0.6× remaining days so the math stays honest.
03
Leave Simulator
Built around Chitkara's actual leave rules — not a generic calculator. Simulate Full Day DL (per subject by classes held), Partial DL (checkbox per slot overlap), or Medical Leave (+5 global). See the impact before you apply.
Medical Leave +5 all subjects
Full Day DL per subject
Partial DL slot overlap
04
Smart Alerts
Color coded status per subject — safe, warning, danger, debar — with a specific action message. Not just a number, but what to do about it.
05
Export, Import, Share
JSON backup, one-tap restore, and a shareable URL that encodes your entire subject list as base64. No account, no server, no sync needed.
