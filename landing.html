Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built by CSE-3 students, for CSE-3 students.

🏥
DL simulator
Enter your DL time window with a real time-picker — Presynce checks it against your timetable and credits the right classes as attended.
📊
Real-time calculations
Every card shows current %, how many you can miss, how many to attend to recover, and exact impact per skip — recalculated from your actual ratio.
🚫
Didn't Go Today
One tap marks every class on today's timetable absent — labs correctly count as two missed classes, not one.
🔔
Class-end reminders
Turn on notifications and get nudged the moment a class from your timetable ends, so logging attendance never slips your mind.
🔮
End-sem prediction
Best-case and worst-case predictions for end of semester, based on remaining classes and your current ratio.
📱
PWA — install it
Add to home screen on Android or iOS. Works fully offline once cached. Optional Google sign-in syncs your data across devices — everything still works without one.
The Math

Chitkara's actual formula

Every calculation uses the real percentage-based formula — not a linear approximation.

ActionFormulaResult
Current75 ÷ 80 × 10093.75%
Skip 175 ÷ 81 × 10092.59% (−1.16%)
Attend 176 ÷ 81 × 10093.83% (+0.08%)

Impact scales with total classes — at 50 classes, skipping costs ~2%. At 100 classes, ~1%. Attending gains less than skipping costs because the denominator increases in both cases.

Why we built this

Chalkpad shows numbers.
It doesn't tell you what they mean.

Every student manually calculates: "I have 32/44. That's 72.7%. I need 75. How many classes do I need to attend?" We built Presynce so that calculation is instant, automatic, and timetable-aware.

Built in April–May 2026 as a first-year CSE project at Chitkara University, by Bhavya Bansal and Pranavi Salwan.
